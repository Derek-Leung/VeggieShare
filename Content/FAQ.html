1. What is Preppy?

    Preppy is a food planning, mobile web application that allows you to browse recipes and find one to your suiting. Then, you have the option to specify the serving size (small, medium or large) and the number of servings to be cooked. Given this information, Preppy will be able to provide you with the correct and accurate amount of ingredients needed to produce your meal! This ensures no extra food will be purchased and wasted!

  2. What led to the creation of Preppy?

    In the Metro Vancouver region, leftovers make up 20% of avoidable food waste. Avoidable food waste is food that could have been eaten, but was thrown away or composted instead, including uneaten leftovers or food that spoiled before it was used. Preppy is an application that aims to reduce food waste by providing a good estimate of how much ingredients you need for a specific serving size given a recipe you choose.

  3. How much food waste can we prevent?

    Over 50% of food waste is avoidable! The most common forms of food waste occurs when we buy too much and either cook too much or throw away what we couldn't use! Preppy aims to target these mistakes!

  4. How much money do we lose from excess food per year?

    Canadian households on average lose out on around $700 per year on excess, avoidable food waste. Imagine having $700 extra dollars in your wallets at the end of each year!
